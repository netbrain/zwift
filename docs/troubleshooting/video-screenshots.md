## Symptoms

- Zwift freezes or crashes during a ride
- Crashes often occur when receiving awards or achievements
- More common on AMD/Radeon GPUs

## Solution

Disable video screenshots in Zwift settings:

1. Open Zwift
2. Go to **Settings** (menu icon in bottom left)
3. Disable **Video Screenshots**

You may also want to set **Auto Upload Screenshots** to "Never" as an extra
precaution.
